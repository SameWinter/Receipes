How to make Lasagna

For this reciepe, we are essentially making thich, meaty tomato sauce and layering that with noodles and cheese into a casserole. Here's the run-down:

  1. Start by making the sauce with gorund beed, bell peppers, onions and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste and crushed tomatoes. The three kinds of tomatoes goves the sauce great depth of flavour.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready. We're using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesa -- like the mix of tomatoes, this 3-chesse blend gives the lasagna great flavour!
  3. From there, it's kust an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese. Repeat until you have three layers of cheese and have used up all the ingredients.
  4. Bake until nubbly and you're ready to eat!
